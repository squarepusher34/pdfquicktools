FAQ

Is PDFQuickTools free?

Yes, every tool is completely free with no limits.

Are files uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs right in your browser — your files never leave your device.

Is it safe?

Yes. Your files are never stored or shared with third parties.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes, it works smoothly on phones and tablets.

Do I need to install anything?

No, there's nothing to download. It's entirely web-based.

Will it damage my PDF files?

No. Your original file stays untouched — only a new output is created.

Is there a file size limit?

Very large files may take longer depending on your browser's performance, but no specific limit is enforced.

Does it work without internet?

Some steps can keep running once the page has loaded, but an internet connection is required the first time you open it.
